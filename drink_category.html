Iced Coffee
Our iced coffee starts with an artful blend of coffees from some of our favorite farms in Colombia and East Africa. These coffees are carefully selected and roasted to be steeped long and cold for a super-smooth flavor. This is cold brew done the way we strive to do everything—patiently, diligently, letting time take its course.
Milkshakes
A milkshake is a sweet, cold beverage that is usually made from milk, ice cream, and/or ice milk, and sometimes flavorings or sweeteners such as butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate syrup, or fruit syrup.
Iced Cappuccino
With less milk than a latte, Iced Cappuccino offers a stronger espresso flavor, a luxurious texture and a velvety, frothy foam with a crisp, cool undercurrent.
Fruit Smoothie
This frozen fruit smoothie is an icy blend of ice pieces and fresh fruit. An easy nutrient packed drink that’s perfect for a hot day.
Tea
Tea is an aromatic beverage commonly prepared by pouring hot or boiling water over cured leaves. There are many different types of tea, such as peppermint, apple cinnamon, honey lemon, and green tea.
Coffee
Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain Coffee species.
Latte
A typical latte is made with six to eight ounces of steamed milk and one shot of espresso.he beverage may also be topped with foam for both flavor and presentation.
French Vanilla
French Vanilla is a creamy blend of vanilla and cream is perfect for a luxury coffee drink.
Hot Chocolate
Hot chocolate, also known as drinking chocolate, cocoa, and as chocolate tea in Nigeria, is a heated drink consisting of shaved chocolate, melted chocolate or cocoa powder, heated milk or water, and usually a sweetener. Hot chocolate may be topped with whipped cream or marshmallows.
Cappuccino
Cappuccino is a coffee drink that today is typically composed of double espresso and hot milk, with the surface topped with foamed milk.The top third of the drink consists of milk foam; this foam can be decorated with artistic drawings made with the same milk.
